Morning Protocol

Before work begins

Use these prompts to set ownership, challenge, and direction before the day starts running you.

  • What is one thing I own today?
  • What will I do that scares me just a little?
  • What is my win condition for today?
Evening Protocol

Before the day closes

End the day by reviewing reality, not by numbing out. Clarity compounds when you tell yourself the truth.

  • What went well?
  • What did I avoid, and why?
  • What is one courageous act I am proud of?
How To Use It

Keep it simple. Keep it daily.

  1. Print the page or copy the prompts into your notes app.
  2. Complete it every day without negotiating with yourself.
  3. Review your responses weekly and look for patterns.
  4. Use those patterns to adjust your training, business, and mindset focus.

The value is not in perfection. The value is in repetition, honesty, and what those two reveal over time.

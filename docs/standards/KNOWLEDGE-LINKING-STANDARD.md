# Knowledge Linking Standard

Version: 1.0.0

Status:
Active Standard

------------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Purpose

Knowledge compounds through relationships.

Every significant document should intentionally connect to related concepts.

The objective is to build a navigable body of knowledge rather than isolated documents.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Required Sections

Every major document should conclude with:

---

## Related Concepts

List closely related concepts within the Discipline.

Example:

- Human Operating System
- Stewardship
- Body Code
- Systems Philosophy

---

## Related Research

Reference supporting research.

---

## Related Case Studies

Reference practical implementations.

---

## Related Standards

Reference governing standards.

---

## Continue Reading

Provide three to five recommended next documents.

Knowledge should encourage exploration.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Linking Principle

A document should never become a dead end.

Every document should become an entry point into the Discipline.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Reader Principle

The reader should always know:

Where they are.

How this document fits.

Where they should go next.

# Progressive Disclosure Standard

Version: 1.0.0

Status:
Active Standard

------------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Purpose

The Systems Architect Discipline should be understandable to newcomers while remaining deep enough for advanced study.

Every major document should reveal knowledge in layers.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Principle

Do not force the reader to understand everything at once.

Start with clarity.

Then provide depth.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Standard Structure

Major documents should generally include:

1. Overview

2. Core Idea

3. Practical Meaning

4. Deeper Explanation

5. Related Research

6. Related Concepts

7. Continue Reading

------------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Reader Levels

### New Reader

Needs orientation.

### Practitioner

Needs application.

### Researcher

Needs evidence.

### Contributor

Needs standards and context.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Rule

Every page should help the reader understand:

Where am I?

Why does this matter?

How does this connect?

Where should I go next?

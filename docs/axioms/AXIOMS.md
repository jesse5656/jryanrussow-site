# Systems Architect Axioms

Version: 0.1.0
Status: Draft

## Axiom 0

Knowledge that cannot be transferred cannot compound.

## Axiom 1

Everything naturally trends toward disorder unless intentionally maintained.

## Axiom 2

The quality of a person's life is constrained by the quality of the systems that support it.

## Axiom 3

Systems that are not intentionally designed will eventually be designed by circumstance.

## Axiom 4

Every generation either inherits systems or inherits the cost of rebuilding them.

## Axiom 5

Freedom is proportional to the quality of the systems that protect time, energy, knowledge, and judgment.

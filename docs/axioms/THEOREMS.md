# Theorems

Version: 0.1.0

Status:
Working Draft

------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Theorem 1

The quality of a person's life is constrained by the quality of the systems
that support it.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Theorem 2

Systems that are not intentionally designed will eventually be designed by
circumstance.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Theorem 3

Every generation either inherits systems or inherits the cost of rebuilding
them.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Theorem 4

Knowledge that cannot be transferred cannot compound.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Theorem 5

No system can permanently outperform the character, competence, and
commitment of its stewards.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Theorem 6

Institutional durability depends upon successful stewardship transfer.

Complete

Major deliverables:

- Constitution
- Canon Structure
- Canon Maturity Model
- Research Workflow
- Systems Architect Research Cycle
- Knowledge Linking Standard
- Progressive Disclosure Standard
- Writing Standard

The Discipline is moving from:

Architecture

to

Scholarship

- evidence collection
- case studies
- research papers
- framework development
- Canon advancement

over additional governance documents.

Status:
Active

- Stewardship

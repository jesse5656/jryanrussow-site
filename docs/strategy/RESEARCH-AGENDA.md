## Purpose

This document identifies the major research priorities of the Systems Architect Discipline.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Research Program 001 — Institutional Memory

Primary Question:

Why do organizations repeatedly lose knowledge they have already paid to acquire?

Focus Areas:

- organizational forgetting
- tribal knowledge
- documentation failure
- knowledge transfer
- institutional resilience
- enterprise memory infrastructure

------------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Future Research Programs

### Stewardship

How do individuals and organizations responsibly preserve and improve what they inherit?

### Human Operating System

How do people develop the capability required for sustained stewardship?

### Business Operating System

How do organizations become less dependent on individual heroics and more capable over time?

### Knowledge Operating System

How should knowledge be captured, structured, linked, retrieved, taught, and preserved?

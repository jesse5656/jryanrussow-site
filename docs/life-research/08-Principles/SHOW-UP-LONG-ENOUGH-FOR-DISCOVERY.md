# Show Up Long Enough for Discovery

Status:
Candidate Principle

------------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Observation

Several major turning points occurred only because the work continued after immediate progress was not visible.

Examples include:

- Recovery
- Sleep apnea diagnosis
- Strength training
- Wrestling coaching
- Business systems
- Research

------------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Working Principle

Consistency does not guarantee success.

Consistency preserves the opportunity for discovery.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Research Needed

Determine whether this principle is supported across psychology, organizational learning, elite performance, and systems engineering.

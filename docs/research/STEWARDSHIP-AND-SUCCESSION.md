# Stewardship and Succession

Version: 0.1.0
Status: Research Question

## Core Question

How does a Systems Architect determine when to continue investing in a system and when stewardship has become so misaligned that it is wiser to build elsewhere?

## Working Hypothesis

Systems require stewards.

A technically sound system may still fail when transferred to people who lack the character, competence, commitment, or incentives required to maintain it.

## Key Questions

- What makes a steward trustworthy?
- What makes a system transferable?
- What minimum culture is required for continuity?
- How should governance protect against low-character leadership?
- When is leaving a system an act of wisdom rather than abandonment?
- How should succession be designed before the founder exits?

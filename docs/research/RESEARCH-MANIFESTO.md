# Research Manifesto

Version: 1.0.0

------------------------------------------------------------------------------

We do not begin with conclusions.

We begin with observations.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------

We do not protect ideas.

We test ideas.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------

We do not seek novelty.

We seek usefulness.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------

We do not fear criticism.

We welcome it.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------

We do not write to impress.

We write to preserve.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------

We do not create frameworks because they are elegant.

We create them because they help people.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Every generation deserves to begin further ahead than the last.

That is why this research exists.

# Systems Architect Research Cycle

Version: 1.0.0

Status:
Active Standard

------------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Purpose

The Systems Architect Discipline advances through continuous observation, research, implementation, and refinement.

Research is not a one-time activity.

It is a permanent operating cycle.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Stage 1 — Observation

Observe reality without forcing conclusions.

Capture:

- problems
- successes
- failures
- conversations
- organizations
- people
- systems
- decisions

Questions are more valuable than immediate answers.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Stage 2 — Capture

Preserve observations before memory changes them.

Sources include:

- Field Research
- Life Research
- Reading
- Interviews
- AI conversations
- Operational reviews
- Historical documents

------------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Stage 3 — Pattern Recognition

Review observations over time.

Look for:

- recurring failures
- recurring successes
- hidden assumptions
- leverage points
- incentives
- constraints

Patterns become research questions.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Stage 4 — Research

Seek evidence.

Challenge assumptions.

Look for contradictory evidence.

Strengthen understanding.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Stage 5 — Framework Development

Only after sufficient evidence should frameworks be created.

Frameworks should simplify reality.

They should never distort reality.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Stage 6 — Implementation

Apply frameworks in:

- MIDWESTGuard
- Midwest24
- Personal life
- Teaching
- Coaching
- Research

Implementation produces new observations.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Stage 7 — Validation

Evaluate outcomes honestly.

Did capability improve?

Did resilience improve?

Did stewardship improve?

Did understanding improve?

If not, revise.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Stage 8 — Canon

When an idea survives repeated implementation and revision, it may enter the Canon.

The Canon records current best understanding.

Not permanent truth.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Continuous Principle

Research never ends.

Every implementation becomes the beginning of the next observation.

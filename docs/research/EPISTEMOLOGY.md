# Epistemology

Version: 0.1.0

Status:
Living Document

------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Purpose

This document defines how knowledge is evaluated within the Russow Institute.

The Institute seeks truth over originality.

Ideas are continually refined through observation,
experience,
research,
criticism,
and implementation.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Knowledge Categories

OBSERVATION

A repeated real-world experience.

May be true.

Requires further validation.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------

PATTERN

Multiple observations producing similar outcomes.

Worth documenting.

Still open to challenge.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------

PRINCIPLE

A broadly applicable truth supported by repeated evidence,
experience, and logical consistency.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------

FRAMEWORK

A structured method for applying one or more principles.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------

HYPOTHESIS

A proposed explanation that requires testing.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------

MODEL

A representation of reality.

Useful models may remain incomplete.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------

LAW

A principle that has remained consistently true across domains
and repeated observation.

The Institute should adopt very few Laws.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Rule of Humility

Every idea may improve.

Evidence outranks ego.

## Preamble

The Russow Institute exists to preserve and advance knowledge that helps
people intentionally build better lives, stronger businesses,
healthier families, and enduring institutions.

We believe systems are among humanity's greatest tools for creating freedom,
stewardship, resilience, and compounding value.

Everything created by this Institute should ultimately contribute to that purpose.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Article I
Purpose

The purpose of the Institute is to discover,
develop,
test,
document,
teach,
and continually improve Systems Architecture.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Article II
Mission

Build systems that preserve what matters.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Article III
Vision

Develop a body of work that continues helping people long after its
founder is gone.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Article IV
Core Values

Stewardship

Truth

Humility

Discipline

Responsibility

Continuous Improvement

Generational Thinking

Freedom Through Systems

------------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Article V
Decision Filter

Every project should answer YES to these questions.

Does it strengthen people?

Does it preserve knowledge?

Does it reduce unnecessary friction?

Does it compound over time?

Does it create meaningful freedom?

Does it align with the Systems Architect Philosophy?

If not,
it should be reconsidered.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Article VI
Long-Term Objective

Create one of the world's most practical and enduring bodies of work on
Systems Architecture.

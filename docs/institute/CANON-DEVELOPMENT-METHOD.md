Foundational Method

------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Purpose

The Canon does not begin with ideas.

It begins with reality.

The purpose of this method is to ensure that every principle,
framework,
and chapter of the Systems Architect Discipline can be traced back
to lived experience, observation, research, and continual refinement.

Principles

Reality is the highest authority.

Experience is data.

Memory is evidence,
not proof.

Primary sources strengthen memory.

Research challenges assumptions.

Frameworks explain patterns.

The Canon preserves the current best understanding.

Nothing skips steps.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Rule

The farther an idea moves away from reality,
the stronger the evidence required to support it.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Mission

Transform lived experience into transferable wisdom
through disciplined observation,
research,
testing,
and stewardship.

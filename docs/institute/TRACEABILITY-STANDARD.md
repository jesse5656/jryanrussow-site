# Traceability Standard

Version: 1.0.0

Status:
Active Institute Standard

------------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Purpose

The Systems Architect Discipline exists to discover, preserve, apply, and transfer capability.

Capability cannot be trusted unless its origin is understood.

Every significant idea should therefore be traceable to the observations, evidence, and research that produced it.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------

## First Principle

Every important idea must be traceable to its origin.

## Why Traceability Matters

Traceability:

- preserves institutional memory
- improves scientific rigor
- reduces rediscovery
- enables revision
- supports reproducibility
- protects against unsupported conclusions

------------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Research Rule

No Canon principle should exist without documented lineage.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Software Rule

Midwest24 Archive should ultimately preserve these relationships natively.

Knowledge should be represented as a connected graph rather than isolated documents.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Stewardship Rule

Future generations should be able to determine:

Where an idea originated.

How it evolved.

What evidence supports it.

What evidence challenged it.

Why it was accepted.

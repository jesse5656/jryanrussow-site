# Anti-Dogma Charter

Version: 0.1.0

Status:
Foundational Governance

------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Purpose

The Russow Institute exists to pursue truth,
not to defend ideas.

No framework,
definition,
model,
or principle is beyond revision.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Commitments

We seek truth over originality.

We seek usefulness over popularity.

We seek clarity over complexity.

We seek evidence over certainty.

We seek stewardship over ownership.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Every important idea must remain open to:

Criticism

Replication

Testing

Improvement

Replacement

------------------------------------------------------------------------------

The Institute rejects:

Dogmatism

Hero worship

Intellectual arrogance

Frameworks that cannot be challenged

Ideas protected by authority alone

------------------------------------------------------------------------------

The Institute recognizes:

Reality is the final authority.

# Revision Standard

Version: 0.1.0

Purpose

Knowledge improves through revision.

Revision is expected.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Revision Triggers

New evidence

Better definitions

Improved clarity

Additional case studies

Contradictory observations

Framework simplification

------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Every Revision Should Answer

What changed?

Why?

What evidence supported the change?

What assumptions changed?

Does this affect other frameworks?

------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Revision History should become part of every major document.

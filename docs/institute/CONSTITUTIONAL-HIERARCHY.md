# Constitutional Hierarchy

Version: 1.0.0

Status:
Foundational Governance

------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Purpose

Not all knowledge has equal authority.

This hierarchy defines the authority of every document within the
Systems Architect Discipline.

When conflicts arise, documents higher in the hierarchy take precedence.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------

LEVEL 0

Reality

Reality is the ultimate authority.

If reality contradicts the Canon,
the Canon must change.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------

LEVEL 1

The Constitution

Defines the mission, purpose, and values of the discipline.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------

LEVEL 2

Definitions

The official language of the discipline.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------

LEVEL 3

Axioms

Foundational assumptions.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------

LEVEL 4

Principles

Broad truths supported by evidence and observation.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------

LEVEL 5

Frameworks

Practical implementations of principles.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------

LEVEL 6

Case Studies

Applications of frameworks in reality.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------

LEVEL 7

Field Research

Observations that may eventually improve the discipline.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------

LEVEL 8

Publications

Books

Courses

Articles

Talks

Software

AI

Websites

All publications derive from higher levels.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Rule

Nothing may contradict a higher level without revising the higher level first.

# Writing Standards

Every document should pursue clarity over cleverness.

The repository exists to preserve knowledge, not impress readers.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Every major topic should answer:

What is it?

Why does it matter?

How does it work?

How is it implemented?

How is it improved?

------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Definitions precede frameworks.

Frameworks precede implementation.

Implementation precedes optimization.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Write from experience whenever possible.

Support claims with research whenever appropriate.

Clearly distinguish:

Observation

Principle

Hypothesis

Opinion

Research

------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Whenever possible use:

Story

↓

Observation

↓

Principle

↓

Framework

↓

Application

------------------------------------------------------------------------------

The objective is not to sound intelligent.

The objective is to become understandable.

# The Russow Learning Cycle

Version: 0.1.0

Status:
Working Observation

------------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Purpose

This document captures a recurring learning pattern observed across multiple decades of experience.

It is an observation, not yet a validated framework.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Initial Observation

Across construction, entrepreneurship, recovery, leadership, strength training, coaching, and research, the same cycle appears repeatedly.

Rather than immediately accepting conventional wisdom or abandoning difficult problems, the process tends to follow a consistent sequence.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Observed Learning Cycle

Encounter Reality

↓

Remain Present

↓

Observe Carefully

↓

Seek Better Systems

↓

Implement

↓

Measure

↓

Refine

↓

Teach Others

↓

Preserve Knowledge

↓

Repeat

Capability grows most rapidly when difficult experiences are intentionally converted into documented knowledge and repeatable systems.

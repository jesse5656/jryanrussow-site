## Context

Recovery required rebuilding far more than physical strength.

It required rebuilding discipline, habits, judgment, identity, and long-term
thinking.

The process demonstrated that physical health cannot be separated from the
other systems that govern human capability.

## What Happened

Recovery was not one decision.

It became thousands of small decisions repeated over time.

No single action transformed the outcome.

Compounding did.

## What I Believed Before

Fitness was primarily physical.

## What I Believed After

The body is an operating system.

Sleep.

Nutrition.

Strength.

Movement.

Recovery.

Stress.

Environment.

Mindset.

All interact continuously.

The whole system determines capability.

## Emerging Principle

Health is not a goal.

Health is infrastructure.

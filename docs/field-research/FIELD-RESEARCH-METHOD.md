# Field Research Method

Version: 0.1.0

Status:
Draft Method

## Purpose

The Field Research Method converts lived experience into transferable knowledge.

## Process

Experience

↓

Observation

↓

Pattern

↓

Question

↓

Research

↓

Principle

↓

Framework

↓

Canon

## Entry Standard

Every field research entry should identify:

- Context
- Decision
- Outcome
- Lesson
- Possible principle
- Related framework
- Evidence strength
- Open questions

## Rule

Experience is not automatically truth.

Experience becomes useful when it is examined, compared, tested, and refined.

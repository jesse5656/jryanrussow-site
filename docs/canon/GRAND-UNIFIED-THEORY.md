# Grand Unified Theory

Version: 0.1.0

Status:
Foundational Research

------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Purpose

This document attempts to identify the single organizing principle that
explains the Systems Architect Discipline.

It should remain under continual revision.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Research Question

What is the most fundamental idea from which the entire discipline emerges?

------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Current Working Model

Time is finite.

↓

Because time is finite,
stewardship becomes necessary.

↓

Because stewardship is necessary,
systems become valuable.

↓

Because systems create repeatability,
knowledge can be transferred.

↓

Because knowledge can be transfered,
human capability compounds.

↓

Because capability compounds,
future generations inherit greater opportunity.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Current Hypothesis

The purpose of Systems Architecture is to intentionally preserve and compound
human capability across generations.

Everything else in the discipline should ultimately support or challenge
this hypothesis.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Open Questions

Is stewardship the true organizing principle?

Or is there an even deeper principle?

Should human flourishing replace human capability?

Should wisdom replace capability?

Should responsibility replace stewardship?

The discipline remains open to revision.

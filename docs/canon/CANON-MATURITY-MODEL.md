# Canon Maturity Model

Version: 1.0.0

Status:
Active Standard

------------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Purpose

The Systems Architect Canon is developed incrementally.

Documents mature through defined stages.

No document should be considered permanent.

Reality remains the highest authority.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Stage 1

Concept

Characteristics

- Idea captured
- Minimal structure
- Research beginning
- Questions exceed answers

------------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Stage 2

Foundational Draft

Characteristics

- Complete first draft
- Major concepts introduced
- Internal consistency reviewed
- Ready for discussion

------------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Stage 3

Reviewed Draft

Characteristics

- Multiple revisions completed
- Cross references added
- Supporting research incorporated
- Writing quality improved

------------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Stage 4

Field Validated

Characteristics

- Tested in real organizations
- Tested through teaching
- Tested through implementation
- Feedback incorporated

------------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Stage 5

Canon

Characteristics

- Stable
- Mature
- Extensively reviewed
- Supported by research
- Historically preserved

------------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Guiding Principle

Nothing enters the Canon because it sounds good.

It enters the Canon because it survives reality.

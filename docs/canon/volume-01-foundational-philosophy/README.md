# Volume I — Foundational Philosophy

Version: 0.1.0

Status:
Working Draft

Purpose:
Define the foundational philosophy of the Systems Architect Discipline.

Core idea:
Systems Architecture exists to help people intentionally preserve and compound human capability across generations.

This volume will establish:

- Why systems matter
- Why stewardship matters
- Why time matters
- Why knowledge must be transferred
- Why systems fail
- Why human capability must be preserved

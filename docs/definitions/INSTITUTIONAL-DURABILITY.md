# Institutional Durability

Version: 0.1.0
Status: Draft Definition

## Working Definition

Institutional durability is the ability of a system, organization, or body of knowledge to continue functioning, improving, and preserving its purpose after its original founder, architect, or steward is no longer directly leading it.

- Succession
- Governance
- Culture

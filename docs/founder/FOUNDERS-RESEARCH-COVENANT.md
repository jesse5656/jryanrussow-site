# Founder's Research Covenant

Version: 1.0.0

------------------------------------------------------------------------------

I recognize that memory is imperfect.

I recognize that experience is valuable,
but not infallible.

I commit to pursuing reality over ego.

I will allow evidence to challenge my beliefs.

I will revise frameworks when better understanding emerges.

I will distinguish observation from interpretation.

I will preserve failures alongside successes.

I will not exaggerate for inspiration.

I will not simplify reality merely to make ideas easier to teach.

I will pursue stewardship above recognition.

The purpose of this work is not to prove that I was right.

The purpose is to leave future generations better equipped than I was.

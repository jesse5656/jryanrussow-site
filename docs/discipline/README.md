## Purpose

The Systems Architect Discipline is the body of knowledge concerned with
the intentional design, documentation, implementation, transfer, and
stewardship of systems that preserve what matters and create compounding
freedom over time.

The Russow Institute exists to steward, research, refine, and teach this discipline.

The Institute serves the discipline.

The discipline does not exist merely to serve the Institute.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Core Question

How do we intentionally preserve and compound human capability across generations?

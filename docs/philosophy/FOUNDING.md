# Founding

The Russow System was not created in 2026.

It began around 1993 when a ten-year-old boy started cleaning
and rebuilding appliances with his grandfather.

The ideas that eventually became the Human Operating System,
Business Operating System, Knowledge Operating System,
The Three-Pillar Method, and The Compound Performance Circle
were forged over decades through entrepreneurship,
construction, leadership, injury, recovery,
fatherhood, technology, documentation,
and the relentless pursuit of systems
that preserve what matters most.

This repository exists to preserve those ideas,
refine them,
challenge them,
and ultimately pass them forward.

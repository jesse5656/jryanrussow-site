# J Ryan Russow Editorial Standards
Last Updated: 2026-07-05

---

# Mission

Every article should leave the reader stronger, more capable, and better equipped to lead.

We do not publish content merely to rank in search engines.

We publish practical frameworks built from real experience.

---

# Core Principles

Write from experience.

Never exaggerate.

Teach practical systems.

Support opinions with reasoning.

Challenge readers respectfully.

Avoid clickbait.

Avoid empty motivation.

Provide actionable takeaways.

---

# Voice

Professional

Confident

Calm

Direct

Evidence-driven

Optimistic

Disciplined

---

# Writing Style

Short paragraphs.

Strong headings.

Clear examples.

Minimal jargon.

Plain English.

Avoid unnecessary buzzwords.

---

# Every Article Should Include

A compelling introduction.

A real-world example.

A practical framework.

Key takeaways.

Frequently Asked Questions.

Internal links.

Author box.

Call to action.

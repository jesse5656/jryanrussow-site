The 3 Pillars of Mental Resilience

  • Identity Shifts — Stop saying "I'm trying." Start saying "I am someone who shows up."
  • Discomfort Training — Cold showers, hard conversations, early mornings — not for suffering, but for signal calibration. You're not building tolerance. You're building response fidelity.
  • Daily Anchors — 5 minutes of journaling: "What went well?" / "One courageous act today?" No fluff. Just data.

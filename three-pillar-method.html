Most financial advice assumes you already have savings. This one doesn't. The Three-Pillar Method™ starts with cash flow — because you can't invest what you don't have. No debt. No luck. Just sequence.

Pillar 1: The Cornerstone Strategy™

Generate active income with a low-cost service business — no experience needed.

  • Lawn care, pressure washing, cleaning, handyman — all start under \$1,000
  • First client strategy: "First home free" for referrals
  • Goal: \$500–\$2,000/month profit in 90 days

Pillar 2: The Short-Term Growth Engine™

Use those profits to buy income-producing real estate — not speculation. Only if it passes the 5-Point Rental Scorecard:

  1. Positive cash flow (\$100+/mo after all expenses)
  2. Safe, growing area
  3. Easy to maintain (no major repairs)
  4. Can be managed remotely or self-managed
  5. Appreciation potential > inflation
